# How to Share a Secret
URL: https://dl.acm.org/doi/10.1145/359168.359176
Authors: Adi Shamir

## Abstract

In this paper we show how to divide data $D$ into $n$ pieces in such a way that $D$ is easily reconstructable from any $k$ pieces, but even complete knowledge of $k - 1$ pieces reveals absolutely no information about $D$. This technique enables the construction of robust key management schemes for cryptographic systems that can function securely and reliably even when misfortunes happen to some of the pieces.
