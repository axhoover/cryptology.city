# Candidate Indistinguishability Obfuscation and Functional Encryption for All Circuits

URL: https://eprint.iacr.org/2013/451
Authors: Sanjam Garg, Craig Gentry, Shai Halevi, Mariana Raykova, Amit Sahai, Brent Waters

## Abstract

In this work, we study indistinguishability obfuscation and functional encryption for general circuits. We give candidate constructions for both primitives. We prove that if these candidates are secure, then a number of open problems in cryptography are resolved, including the problem of constructing functional encryption schemes for arbitrary circuits, public-key encryption with chosen-ciphertext security from only one-way functions, and the construction of deniable encryption. The candidate constructions are based on multilinear maps.
